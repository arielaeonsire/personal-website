NAME: Celestial Parasite
DATE: 2024-08-25
TAGS: cosmic horror, animate star, apocalypse
In cosmic depths there stood a realm of might,
A civ'lisation bathed in light.
With knowledge vast, they reached the peak,
Their cultivated servants meek.

A shielded world, by titans kept.
Their star, source of the life they held.
From universe's birth it seemed to shine,
Eternal, vast, a cosmic sign.

`The star's immortal,' they assumed,
`How else hath it so much withstood?'
They were correct, to their misfortune,
Yet ignorance had taken root.

As death approached - the star's demise,
The civ'lisation had devised
A plan to leave their system's span.
Their downfall came afore they ran.

The burning giant, near its death,
Prepared not for its last breath.
Instead revived with newfound might,
It drained the life of all in sight.

The planet's essence it consumed,
With power fresh, it once more bloomed.
Its rays engulfed all of creation,
Not hot, but cold in their sensation.

The day brought death, a chilling fear,
With withered corpses covering the land,
The remnants faded, year by year,
And in soon time, there came the end.

A ring of light around their island -
Malevolent illumination found its way.
The dwellers of the night turned silent -
Their suffering became decay.

With size diminishing, the planet stood -
The last of those that ever could.
The star, uncaring, fed with glee,
A planet vanished, lost at sea.
