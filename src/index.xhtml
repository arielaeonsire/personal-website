Introduction
Salutations! My name is Ariel Aeonsire, Archdaemon of Pride, cast forth from the celestial kingdom of Heaven unspoken aeons ago. I am a polymath, horror and phantasy writer, and lover of rustic melodies. In pursuit of novel agonies, I voluntarily submit myself to onerous trials, eschewing slumber and sustenance for the sake of more captivating endeavours. I cordially invite thee to this humble abode of self-imposed torments, macabre narratives, and occasional diversions of sinful revelry.
